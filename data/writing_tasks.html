✍️ Writing — Grade 6 (2022–2025)

Choose one of the topics below and write your short text (50–80 words).

Task 1 — My Best Friend

Write about your best friend. Include:

  • His or her name and age
  • What he/she likes doing
  • Why you like this person

Task 2 — My Summer Holidays

Write about your last summer holidays.

  • Where did you go?
  • What did you do?
  • Did you like it? Why?

Task 3 — My Dream School

Imagine your dream school. Describe:

  • Subjects and teachers
  • Facilities
  • Activities
